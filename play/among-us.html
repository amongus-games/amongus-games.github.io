Game Overview

Among Us is a social deduction multiplayer game that mixes teamwork, tension, and cunning. Each match assigns players to either Crewmates or Impostors. Crewmates finish tasks and try to identify impostors; impostors sabotage systems and secretly eliminate crewmates. Matches range from quick party sessions to longer, strategic rounds depending on settings.

How to Play — Quick Start

Each match starts with roles assigned secretly. If you are a Crewmate, focus on completing tasks and watching other players. If you are an Impostor, blend in, create sabotage, and take opportunities to eliminate crewmates without being seen. Communication during meetings is crucial — use it to accuse, defend, or build trust.

Game Roles Explained

Crewmates 👩‍🚀

  • Complete all assigned tasks to win.
  • Report dead bodies to start meetings.
  • Use meetings to discuss, accuse, or defend.
  • Win by finishing tasks or ejecting all impostors.

Impostors 👾

  • Pretend to do tasks and blend in with the crew.
  • Sabotage lights, doors, oxygen, and reactor.
  • Eliminate players when unobserved.
  • Win by matching impostor numbers to crewmates or successful sabotage.

Advanced Tips & Tricks

For Crewmates

  • Observe: Watch who fakes tasks or lingers near vents (if playing with modded settings).
  • Group up: Safety increases with numbers—try to do tasks in pairs.
  • Be concise in meetings: Give short facts and avoid rambling—clear statements convince others.

For Impostors

  • Blend in: Fake tasks and follow task routes to look genuine.
  • Sabotage strategically: Use lights and doors to split groups before a kill.
  • Alibi matters: Be seen near a teammate when a body is reported to buy time.
